Invisible Item Frame

1.16 +

Adds a Recipe to craft an Invisible Item Frame to make some nice looking buildings.

To see the Item Frame when nothing is in it, it is marked with gray particles. After you put something in the Item Frame, they disappear.

Crafting

The Crafting Table will show a Structure Void as result, but you will get the correct Item after you take it out.
