This Datapack won't be continued due to it is now possible in Minecraft to change the look of Redstone back to a Dot when right clicking it.

If you break a Redstone in Creative Mode, the other Redstones around it will not update, due to a Limitation of Minecraft. In Survival Mode this works without Problems.

Sometimes it is also possible that a Redstone is not converted correctly if the Player moves fast during placement or if the Redstone is placed during the Player looks very slightly past to a Hitbox of a Block.
